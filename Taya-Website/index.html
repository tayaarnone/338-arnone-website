ABOUT ME

Hello viewer! My name is Taya Arnone, and I live to create. In a chaotic world I found order in creation and the making of a nothing into a something. Whether that be capturing a raw moment in time with the closing of a shutter, or simply sketching my imagination onto a blank page, I consider myself to be a photographer, graphic designer, artist, and videographer. I have always been drawn to the arts, and thus was pulled toward the world of graphic design (one that I will not likely come out of). I am constantly inspired by this world and the humans in it and so I strive to contribute to the world in some way with my work. I am currently a junior at Cal Poly majoring in Graphic Communication, living in the beautiful San Luis Obispo. If you would like to contact me, shoot me an email at arnonetaya@gmail.com!
